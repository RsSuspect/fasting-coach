Meals & recipes

Chicken Power Bowl · ~700 kcal

Ingredients

  • 250 g chicken breast
  • 150 g cooked basmati rice
  • 200 g broccoli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika

Method

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Air-fry or bake at 200°C for 18–20 minutes.
  3. Cook rice and steam broccoli.
  4. Serve with olive oil over the vegetables.

Lean Beef & Potatoes · ~800 kcal
  • 200 g 5% lean beef mince
  • 250 g potatoes
  • 200 g green beans
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Onion, garlic, paprika, mixed herbs
  1. Roast diced potatoes at 200°C for 35–40 minutes.
  2. Brown beef with onion, garlic, and spices.
  3. Steam green beans and add olive oil before serving.
Salmon & Potatoes · ~750 kcal
