Chicken Power Bowl · ~700 kcal

Ingredients

  • 250 g chicken breast
  • 150 g cooked basmati rice
  • 200 g broccoli
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika

Method

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Air-fry or bake at 200°C for 18–20 minutes.
  3. Cook rice and steam broccoli.
  4. Serve with olive oil over the vegetables.
Greek Yogurt Protein Bowl · ~300 kcal
  • 300 g 0% Greek yogurt
  • 30 g whey protein
  • 100 g berries
  1. Mix whey into yogurt.
  2. Top with berries and chill.
Lean Beef & Potatoes · ~800 kcal
  • 200 g 5% lean beef mince
  • 250 g potatoes
  • 200 g green beans
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Onion, garlic, paprika, mixed herbs
  1. Roast diced potatoes at 200°C for 35–40 minutes.
  2. Brown beef with onion, garlic, and spices.
  3. Steam green beans and add olive oil before serving.
Salmon & Potatoes · ~750 kcal
  • 180–200 g salmon
  • 250 g baby potatoes
  • Broccoli
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil
  1. Bake salmon at 200°C for 15–18 minutes.
  2. Roast potatoes and steam broccoli.
  3. Finish with lemon.
Chicken Wrap · ~700 kcal
  • 250 g chicken
  • 2 wholemeal wraps
  • Lettuce, tomato, cucumber
  • 30 g light mayonnaise
  1. Cook and slice chicken.
  2. Warm wraps, add salad and chicken, then roll.

Sunday meal prep

  • Cook chicken, rice, potatoes, beef, and vegetables in batches.
  • Portion lunches and dinners for Monday–Friday.
  • Keep yogurt, whey, and berries separate.
